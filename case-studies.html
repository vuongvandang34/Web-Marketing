Nail Salon Local SEO

Signature Nail Lounge: Denver, CO

The Problem: A high-end nail salon offering excellent treatments was completely invisible in local search. Despite a physical location on a busy street, search queries like "nail salon near me" displayed three competitor salons located further away. They averaged just 12 customer calls a week from Maps.

The Strategy: We restructured their primary categories, added secondary categories for specialized services (e.g. pedicure, gel nails, massage), geotagged and optimized interior photos, launched a weekly local posting schedule, and audited and matched directory citations across 30 local indexers.

Performance metrics
Google Maps rank #14 on average
Google Maps rank Rank #1 - #3
Monthly Maps Calls 48 calls / mo
Monthly Maps Calls 368 calls / mo
Review Protection & Removal

Vesuvio Pizzeria: Chicago, IL

The Problem: A family-run pizzeria became the target of a competitor's negative spam attack. Over a 48-hour period, 18 suspicious 1-star reviews spammed their profile from empty accounts, dropping their average rating from 4.7 down to 3.8. Customer bookings plummeted.

The Strategy: We conducted an immediate reputation audit, mapped the fake reviews against Google's policy rules, compiled metadata logs showing reviewer duplication, submitted merchant appeals, and escalated the claims to Google's support team.

Performance metrics
Merchant Rating 3.8 Stars
Merchant Rating 4.7 Stars Restored
Fake Reviews Visible 18 fake reviews
Fake Reviews Visible 3 (15 removed)
Local Google Maps Ads

Express Plumbing: Houston, TX

The Problem: A regional plumbing service provider was spending $1,500 every month on Google search ads. Clicks were expensive ($8-12 each) and they lacked tracking, meaning they had no idea if the ads were actually creating emergency service call bookings.

The Strategy: We restructured their ad campaigns to focus strictly on Google Maps Promoted Pins and Call-Only Ads. We narrowed their geographic radius targeting to high-margin suburban neighborhoods, added a comprehensive negative keyword list, and deployed active call-tracking conversion tags.

Performance metrics
Ad Cost-Per-Call $82 per call
Ad Cost-Per-Call $24 per call
Monthly Leads Booked 18 emergency leads
Monthly Leads Booked 62 emergency leads
