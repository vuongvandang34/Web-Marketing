Google Maps Local SEO Service

Stop losing customer calls to competitors. We optimize your Google Business Profile to rank in the coveted Local 3-Pack when customers search nearby.

Why Your Local Business is Not Ranking in the Top 3

Google Maps ranking isn't random. Google's algorithm ranks profiles based on three primary pillars: Relevance (how well your profile details match the search), Distance (how close your store is to the searcher), and Prominence (how active, trustworthy, and validated your business is online).

If your competitors appear above you, it's typically because your profile lacks category alignment, lacks localized keywords in description fields, contains incomplete service logs, or has low interaction activity. Our Local SEO service is designed to fix every algorithmic ranking factor systematically.

Our Google Business Profile Optimization Checklist

We do not use generic shortcuts. We execute a comprehensive map ranking strategy including:

  • Primary & Secondary Category Optimization: Selecting the exact high-traffic categories matching user intent (e.g. aligning primary "Nail Salon" with secondary "Waxing Hair Removal Service" and "Day Spa").
  • Service & Product Menus: Building out comprehensive lists of services, complete with detailed keyword descriptions and pricing models.
  • Localized Citation Audits: Matching your Business Name, Address, and Phone Number (NAP) across major local index directories (Yelp, Apple Maps, Foursquare, etc.) to establish prominence.
  • Geo-Targeted Content Updates: Crafting monthly posts, questions, and uploading geotagged images highlighting real local customer results to boost profile relevance.
  • Competitor Map pack Analysis: Auditing the top-ranking competitors in your area to determine their keyword density and map citation counts.
