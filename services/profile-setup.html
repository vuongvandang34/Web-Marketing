Google Business Profile Setup & Claims

Claim your spot on Google Maps. We build, claim, verify, and launch fully optimized Google Business Profiles for new or unverified service brands.

Who Needs Google Maps Listing Setup Support?

Launching a Google Maps profile for a brand-new business is no longer as simple as typing an address and clicking submit. Google has heavily restricted new listing creation to combat spam. One incorrect category, phone number typo, or lack of business licensing documentation can cause your profile to be instantly suspended upon creation.

We guide local business owners through the setup process to secure a clean launch, specifically assisting:

  • Brand New Businesses: Salon owners, home service providers, or restaurateurs setting up their digital presence from scratch.
  • Unclaimed Listings: Old profiles auto-generated by Google that need to be officially claimed and secured by the actual business owner.
  • Locked or Suspended listings: Listings that went inactive or were suspended due to category violations or invalid business addresses.

Our Google Maps Listing Setup Checklist

We configure every profile parameter accurately to build a high-authority listing from day one:

  • Profile Creation & Claiming: Finding historical auto-listings and submitting claims, or constructing a clean new profile.
  • Google Verification Preparation: Guiding you step-by-step through Google's strict verification requirements. Whether Google requires postcard, phone, or live video verification (displaying store signs, trucks, and business documents), we prep you to pass on the first try.
  • Accurate NAP Configuration: Setting up your Business Name, Address, and Phone number to match your local utility bills and tax filings exactly, preventing future suspensions.
  • Basic SEO Alignment: Populating opening hours, payment methods, accessibility tags, customized business descriptions, and a high-conversion booking CTA link.
  • Visual Asset Uploads: Uploading properly structured logo files, interior/exterior office shots, and service catalogs.

What We Do After Verification

Verifying your profile is only the first step. To rank in Google Maps searches, your listing needs immediate online authority. Upon verification, we immediately deploy:

  1. Launch Citation Bundle: Submitting your business data to the top 20 local directory indexers to establish web prominence.
  2. Review Invite System: Providing simple QR cards and SMS template links to let you collect review feedback from initial clients.
  3. Welcome Post: Publishing your first optimized update announcement post targeting local primary keywords.
