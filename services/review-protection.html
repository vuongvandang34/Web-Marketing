Review Protection & Removal Service

Protect your brand from malicious or fraudulent online spams. We flag and appeal policy-violating ratings to restore trust on your Google Business Profile.

Which Bad Reviews Can Actually Be Removed?

Many local business owners believe Google will never delete negative reviews, or conversely, that they can easily buy tools to delete any complaint. Both assumptions are wrong. Google maintains a strict, policy-based framework for content. We do not use magic tricks; we study and map violating reviews against Google's Prohibited & Restricted Content Policies.

Google Content Standard Violations

Google will remove reviews containing fake reviews (spammed accounts), off-topic rants (political or unrelated to the listing), harassment or hate speech, conflict of interest (left by current/past employees or competitors), or wrong location content.

What Usually Cannot Be Removed

We believe in absolute transparency. We will not take your money for requests that do not meet compliance standards. The following reviews typically cannot be removed:

  • Legitimate Customer Complaints: If a customer visited your shop, had a slow wait, and left a 2-star review detail, Google will not remove it just because you disagree. Legitimate reviews are protected.
  • Polite Negative Feedback: Honest, constructive reviews that outline a negative experience in a professional manner do not violate policies.

For these non-removable reviews, we consult you on ethical review recovery plans: replying professionally, resolving client complaints, and generating positive feedback from happy clients to dilute the rating impact.

Our Policy-Compliant Removal Process

We use a structured, 5-step workflow to report and appeal fake reviews:

  1. Profile Audit: We analyze your listing history and identify suspicious clusters of review spikes.
  2. Evidence Logging: We compile profile metadata: checking reviewer avatar patterns, duplicate review copy left on multiple competitor listings, and timing matches.
  3. Google Compliance Submission: We prepare detailed written arguments citing specific Google policy sections and submit reports via official merchant consoles.
  4. Appeal Escalation: If the initial automated report is denied, we submit formal written appeals to Google support desks presenting our compiled evidence file.
  5. Reputation Shielding: We monitor your listing for new spams and set up alerts to flag fresh attacks immediately.

Legal Compliance & Policy Disclaimer

We only work on reviews that appear to violate platform policies or applicable legal standards. We do not guarantee removal of every negative review. Final decisions are made solely by the Google platform support team. We do not participate in, support, or employ any blackhat rating manipulation, fake review generation, or spamming practices.
