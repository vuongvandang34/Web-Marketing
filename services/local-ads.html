Local Google Ads & Maps Ads

Target buyers looking for your service in your exact neighborhood. We launch hyper-local Maps and Call Campaigns optimized to dial your phone, not just get clicks.

Why Most Local Google Ads Campaigns Fail

Running ads for local service businesses—like nail salons, dental clinics, or plumbing contractors—is completely different from global e-commerce campaigns. Many agencies make the mistake of setting up broad search campaigns, leading to wasted spend on clicks from users located 20 miles away who will never visit your store.

Another common mistake is failing to setup call tracking. Business owners see they spent $500 on clicks, but have no idea if those clicks generated any incoming phone calls or map directions. We build local campaigns focused strictly on Call Conversions and Maps Actions.

How We Set Up High-Converting Maps Campaigns

We optimize your local ad budgets using a structured geographical approach:

  • Tight Radius Targeting: We limit your ad display strictly to a 3-mile or 5-mile radius around your physical shop location, ensuring your budget only targets nearby, high-intent buyers.
  • Google Maps Promoted Pins: We link your Google Ads account with your Google Business Profile to show your store as a green "Promoted Pin" at the top of local Maps searches.
  • Call-Only Ads: Mobile search ads that contain your phone number as the primary CTA, directing users to call your front desk immediately rather than landing on a generic homepage.
  • Negative Location Filters: Excluding surrounding cities, zip codes, or neighborhoods that do not match your ideal client demographics.
  • Call Recording & Lead Verification: Setting up Google conversion tracking tags to monitor call durations and ensure we only optimize ads for high-quality, long-duration inquiries.
