Consumer Behavior

Most local customers decide before visiting your website

When customers need an urgent local service—like booking a salon session, finding a dinner spot, or calling an emergency plumber—they don't browse deep websites. They open Google Maps, type their query, and pick from the top results.

They compare star ratings, review quantities, recent photos, and distance. The business with the strongest local visibility and cleanest reputation wins the customer. If your Maps listing is unoptimized or spammed with negative fake reviews, you lose leads even if your real-world service is far superior.
